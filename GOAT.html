Bugs Bunny -

Bugs bunny is a animated character who is a clever gray hare. He is most known for his role in Looney Tunes. Two recent things he has roles in currently is the new game Multiversus and Space Jam 2. He is a trickster and has great sense of humor and his famous catchphrase is "Eh, what's up, doc?". He has become an American cultural icon and has been featured in films, television series, music, comics, video games, and commercials.
