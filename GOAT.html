Who is the GOAT . . .

Bugs Bunny -

Bugs bunny is a animated character who is a clever gray hare. He is most known for his role in Looney Tunes. Two recent things he has roles in currently is the new game Multiversus and Space Jam 2. He is a trickster and has great sense of humor and his famous catchphrase is "Eh, what's up, doc?". He has become an American cultural icon and has been featured in films, television series, music, comics, video games, and commercials.

Why -

Bugs Bunny is the GOAT because he has appeared in more films that any other animated character. He is an American cultural icon and has his own star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. Bugs Bunny was created around the late 1930s and he is still famous. After all these years he still has the popularity. A great example is that Space Jam 2 came out 2021 and one of the main stars was Bugs Bunny.

History -

On April 30, 1938 Bugs Bunny made his first appearance in a cartoon short called "Porky's Hare Hunt". Even though that was his first appearance his first official Bugs Bunny cartoon was in July 27, 1940. During that cartoon, "A Wild Hare", he uses his famous known catchphrase "What's up, Doc?".
